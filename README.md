Brief Discription of NALAM 360                                                                                                                                                                                                           

Nalam 360 is an AI-powered Rural Healthcare Command Center designed to transform the way India's Primary Health Centres (PHCs) and Community Health Centres (CHCs) manage healthcare resources. Rather than functioning as a traditional hospital management system, Nalam 360 serves as an intelligent decision-support platform that provides healthcare professionals and government authorities with real-time operational visibility, predictive analytics, and AI-driven recommendations.

Public healthcare facilities often struggle with fragmented data, manual record-keeping, delayed reporting, medicine shortages, uneven patient distribution, limited bed visibility, and inefficient workforce planning. These challenges result in reactive decision-making, increased operational costs, and delays in patient care. Nalam 360 addresses these issues by integrating multiple healthcare operations into a single, centralized AI-powered ecosystem that enables proactive healthcare management.

The platform continuously monitors medicine inventory, patient footfall, bed occupancy, doctor and staff availability, and disease trends through a unified dashboard. Using machine learning and predictive analytics, it forecasts medicine demand, predicts patient inflow, estimates bed occupancy, identifies staffing shortages, and detects early disease outbreak patterns. Instead of simply displaying data, Nalam 360 transforms operational information into actionable insights by recommending medicine redistribution between nearby PHCs, optimizing procurement, suggesting staff allocation, identifying the best referral hospitals, and generating early warning alerts for health authorities.

Designed specifically for rural healthcare environments, Nalam 360 supports offline-first functionality, multilingual interfaces, voice-based reporting, and mobile accessibility, ensuring usability even in regions with limited internet connectivity. The platform empowers doctors, nurses, pharmacists, ASHA workers, hospital administrators, and government officials by reducing administrative workload and enabling faster, evidence-based decisions.

By shifting healthcare management from a reactive approach to a predictive and data-driven model, Nalam 360 improves resource utilization, minimizes medicine wastage, reduces patient waiting times, enhances referral efficiency, and strengthens public healthcare delivery. Ultimately, the platform creates a connected healthcare ecosystem where every stakeholder operates with real-time information, enabling better patient outcomes and more resilient rural healthcare infrastructure.

Vision

To build India's most intelligent and connected rural healthcare ecosystem by empowering every Primary Health Centre and Community Health Centre with Artificial Intelligence, enabling predictive healthcare, optimized resource management, and equitable access to quality healthcare for every citizen.

Mission

To transform fragmented rural healthcare operations into one unified AI-powered ecosystem that enables real-time monitoring, predictive decision-making, and seamless collaboration between healthcare workers, hospitals, and government authorities, ensuring timely, efficient, and patient-centric healthcare delivery.

Problem Statement

India's rural healthcare system serves millions of people every day through an extensive network of PHCs and CHCs. Despite significant infrastructure investments, healthcare operations remain fragmented due to manual documentation, disconnected software systems, delayed reporting, and limited real-time visibility into critical resources such as medicines, beds, doctors, and patient demand.

As a result, healthcare decisions are often made only after shortages, overcrowding, or staffing issues have already occurred. This reactive approach reduces operational efficiency, increases costs, and directly impacts the quality and accessibility of patient care.

Solution

Nalam 360 introduces an AI-powered Healthcare Command Center that connects medicines, patients, doctors, beds, and healthcare facilities into one intelligent digital ecosystem. The platform combines real-time monitoring with predictive analytics to forecast demand, optimize healthcare resources, automate operational workflows, and provide actionable recommendations before problems arise.

Instead of simply recording healthcare data, Nalam 360 enables healthcare administrators to anticipate challenges, allocate resources intelligently, and make faster, evidence-based decisions that improve healthcare delivery across rural communities.
